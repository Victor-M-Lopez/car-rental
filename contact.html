Return Policy

In an event of a refuned, the renter may not of had used the vehicle for more than an hour if a full refund is in question. If the vehicle has been in use for more than an hour, then a partial refund is given. The partial refund is calculated by the given price per hour/day divided in half. The refund will be given in two to five business days. In case of any issues aquiring a refund, please call (180) - 332 - 0901.

Vehicle Acceptance Policy

A vehicle is accepted to be rented to users by meeting the criteria below:

  • The Vehicle is under car insurance
  • Title and tags are all up to date
  • The vehicle is under 120k miles
  • The vehicle is up to date with repairs (oil change, coolant, tire change, etc.) and in good condition
